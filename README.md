# Instagram Detailed List Viewer

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is a closed-source app for opening Instagram data export lists and turning them into readable follower, following, and account relationship reports. Use it as an Instagram data export viewer, Instagram followers list analyzer, Instagram following list viewer, and privacy-conscious way to understand your own account data without giving a third-party app your Instagram password.

This public repository contains documentation, release notes, support information, and SEO-friendly reference material for the app. The application source code is proprietary and is not published here.

## What You Can Analyze

- Instagram followers from your official data export
- Instagram following lists from your official data export
- Accounts you follow that do not appear in your follower list
- Followers you do not currently follow back
- Account relationship lists such as pending follow requests, blocked accounts, hidden or restricted accounts, and other lists included in your export
- Saved snapshots from different dates so you can compare how your lists changed over time
- AI-powered summaries that help explain list patterns, cleanup opportunities, and account relationship changes

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is built for people who want a clean way to inspect their own Instagram export files. It is not an Instagram scraper, does not require Instagram login credentials, and is not official Instagram analytics.

## Why This App Exists

Instagram lets you download your account information, but the export can be difficult to inspect by hand. The data is often split across folders, JSON files, and long lists that are hard to search, compare, or explain.

Instagram Detailed List Viewer makes those exports easier to work with:

- Import your Instagram data export instead of connecting your Instagram account.
- Search, sort, and filter long follower and following lists.
- Identify one-way relationships such as following but not followed back.
- Compare exports over time to understand changes without manually copying names into spreadsheets.
- Use AI-powered insights to summarize patterns in the lists you provide.
- Keep source files and report context clear so results are easier to verify.

## Good Use Cases

- Audit who you follow before cleaning up an old account.
- Review follower and following lists after downloading your Instagram information.
- Find accounts you follow that do not appear in your follower export.
- Compare two exports from different dates to see which relationships changed.
- Prepare a personal account review without sharing Instagram credentials.
- Turn raw Instagram JSON or downloaded files into a readable list report.

## Privacy Boundaries

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is designed around files that you provide from your own Instagram data download. It does not claim to access private Instagram metrics, reach, saves, impressions, demographics, ad data, or any official internal Instagram analytics.

If a feature uses AI-powered insights, review the app settings and release notes for whether that processing happens locally or uses an external AI provider. Do not upload files that you are not allowed to process.

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is independent software and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to Instagram, Meta, Facebook, or Threads.
