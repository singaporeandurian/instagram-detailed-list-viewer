# Instagram Follower List Analyzer

Instagram Detailed List Viewer can be used as an Instagram follower list analyzer for your own downloaded Instagram data export.

## What A Follower List Analyzer Does

A follower list analyzer opens the followers file from your Instagram export and makes the list easier to inspect. Instead of reading raw JSON, you can review usernames and related fields in a structured report.

Depending on the data available in your export and app version, the report may help you:

- Count follower records loaded from the export
- Search usernames
- Compare followers against your following list
- Find followers you do not follow back
- Compare follower snapshots from different export dates
- Use AI-powered insights to summarize patterns

## What It Does Not Do

An Instagram follower list analyzer based on your export does not show private Instagram metrics. It does not reveal hidden reach, saves, demographics, ad data, or private information from other accounts.

It also cannot prove a complete historical unfollower timeline from one export alone. To compare changes over time, download and save multiple exports.

## How To Analyze Followers

1. Download your Instagram information from your Instagram account settings.
2. Choose JSON if Instagram offers a format option.
3. Open the export in Instagram Detailed List Viewer.
4. Select the followers list.
5. Search, sort, or filter the loaded accounts.
6. Compare with the following list if you want follow-back reports.

## Useful Questions To Ask

- Which followers are not in my following list?
- Did my follower list change between two exports?
- Are there old or unfamiliar accounts I should review?
- Did the export load the expected number of follower records?
