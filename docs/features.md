# Features

Instagram Detailed List Viewer turns hard-to-read Instagram export files into practical list reports. It is designed for personal account review, creator cleanup, social media organization, and privacy-conscious analysis of your own Instagram data.

## Core Features

## Instagram Data Export Viewer

Open Instagram export files and inspect the account relationship lists in a cleaner interface than raw JSON or HTML.

## Followers List Analyzer

Review follower lists, search by username, and understand the total number of follower records loaded from the export.

## Following List Viewer

Open your following list, filter long account lists, and identify accounts you may want to review.

## Not-Following-Back Report

Compare followers and following to find accounts you follow that do not appear in the follower list from the same export.

## Followers You Do Not Follow Back

Find followers who do not appear in your following list. This helps with account organization and relationship review.

## Snapshot Comparison

Compare exports from different dates to understand changes over time. This is the best way to answer historical questions, because a single Instagram export usually shows current data rather than a complete relationship timeline.

## AI-Powered Insights

Use AI-powered summaries to explain patterns, cleanup opportunities, and list changes in plain language. Important conclusions should be verified against the visible data.

## Source-Aware Reports

Keep the export file context clear so you can understand which data was loaded, which lists were compared, and which files were skipped.

## What The App Does Not Do

- It does not require your Instagram password.
- It does not log into Instagram for you.
- It does not scrape private account data.
- It does not provide official Instagram analytics.
- It does not claim to show private metrics such as reach, saves, impressions, ad data, or demographics.
- It does not guarantee that Instagram's export includes every historical account relationship event.

## Who It Is For

- Personal Instagram users cleaning up old following lists
- Creators reviewing follower and following relationships
- Social media managers organizing their own export data
- Researchers who need a readable view of downloaded account lists
- Anyone who wants an Instagram list viewer without sharing login credentials
