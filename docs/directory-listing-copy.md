## Short Description

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is a closed-source app for analyzing your own Instagram data export. Open follower and following lists, compare account relationships, find accounts not following back, review followers you do not follow back, and summarize list patterns with AI-powered insights. No Instagram login required.

## Long Description

Instagram Detailed List Viewer helps you make sense of your official Instagram data export. Instead of opening raw JSON files or copying usernames into a spreadsheet, you can load follower, following, and account relationship lists into a readable report.

The app is useful for personal account cleanup, creator account organization, not-following-back reports, follower/following comparison, export snapshot comparison, and AI-assisted summaries. It is designed around files you provide from your own Instagram download and does not require your Instagram password.

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is independent software and is not affiliated with Instagram or Meta. It does not provide official Instagram analytics, private account metrics, reach, saves, impressions, ad data, or demographics.
