# Getting Started With Instagram Detailed List Viewer

This guide explains how to use Instagram Detailed List Viewer as an Instagram data export viewer and follower list analyzer.

## 1. Download Your Instagram Data

Start from your official Instagram account settings and request a download of your information. Instagram may offer different export options over time, but the most useful option for list analysis is usually the complete account information export with followers and following data included.

Choose JSON when Instagram offers a format choice. JSON files are usually easier for list tools to parse than HTML pages.

## 2. Save The Export Somewhere You Can Find It

Instagram may deliver your export as a compressed archive. Keep the original archive as a backup, then extract it into a folder such as:

```text
Downloads/instagram-export/
```

Do not rename individual files until after you have confirmed the app can read them.

## 3. Open The Export In The App

Open Instagram Detailed List Viewer and choose the extracted export folder or the specific Instagram list files you want to inspect. The app is designed to help you locate follower, following, and relationship lists such as:

- followers
- following
- pending follow requests
- blocked accounts
- restricted or hidden account lists, when present in the export

File names and folder names can change as Instagram updates its export format, so see [file formats](file-formats.md) if a list does not appear.

## 4. Review The Summary

After import, start with the summary view:

- Total followers loaded
- Total following accounts loaded
- Accounts you follow that do not appear in your follower list
- Followers you do not currently follow back
- Export date or snapshot label
- Any skipped or unreadable files

Treat the report as a view of the export you provided, not a live official Instagram database.

## 5. Search, Filter, And Compare

Use search and filters to narrow long lists by username, display name, relationship type, or timestamp when those fields exist in your export. If you import two exports from different dates, compare snapshots to see which accounts were added, removed, followed, or unfollowed between the two files.

## 6. Use AI-Powered Insights Carefully

AI-powered insights can help summarize patterns, cleanup opportunities, and account relationship changes. Always verify important results against the visible lists and the original export files before making decisions.
