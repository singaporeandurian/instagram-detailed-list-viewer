# Privacy And Security

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is designed for people who want to inspect their own Instagram data export without giving a third-party app their Instagram login credentials.

## Privacy Principles

- Use official Instagram exports instead of Instagram passwords.
- Analyze files you are allowed to process.
- Keep source files visible and verifiable.
- Avoid claiming that exported lists are official live analytics.
- Treat AI-generated summaries as helpers that should be checked against source data.

## No Instagram Login Required

The app is documentation-first around export analysis. You should not need to give the app your Instagram username and password to view downloaded list files.

Never enter your Instagram password into a tool unless you intentionally trust that tool and understand the risk.

## Sensitive Data In Exports

Instagram exports can contain sensitive information. Depending on what you requested, an export may include messages, profile information, comments, likes, account lists, and other personal data.

Before importing an export:

- Use only files you need.
- Avoid sharing the export publicly.
- Store it in a private folder.
- Delete temporary copies when finished.
- Check whether AI features send data to an external provider.

## AI-Powered Insights

AI can help summarize long lists, but AI output may be incomplete or wrong. Before using AI insights:

- Confirm which fields are included in the prompt.
- Check whether processing is local or external.
- Avoid sending sensitive files unless you accept the privacy tradeoff.
- Verify important findings against the loaded list report.

## Not Official Instagram Analytics

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is independent software. It is not affiliated with Instagram or Meta, and it does not provide official internal analytics such as reach, impressions, saves, ad performance, profile visits, or audience demographics.
