# Use Cases

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is useful when you have an Instagram data export and need a clear view of long account lists.

## Personal Account Cleanup

Open your following list and review accounts you no longer want to follow. Search, filter, and sort instead of scrolling through Instagram one account at a time.

## Find Accounts Not Following Back

Compare the follower and following lists from the same export to find accounts you follow that do not appear in your follower list.

This is a list comparison, not private Instagram analytics. It depends on the data included in your export.

## Review Followers You Do Not Follow Back

Find followers who do not appear in your following list. This can help you decide whether to follow back friends, creators, customers, or community accounts.

## Compare Two Instagram Exports

Download exports at different dates and compare them. This can help you understand account relationship changes over time, such as newly followed accounts, removed relationships, and changes in list membership.

## Prepare A Personal Data Review

Use the viewer to inspect what Instagram included in your export. This is helpful when you want to understand your own account data without manually reading raw JSON.

## Creator And Brand Account Organization

Creators and small teams can use the app to review relationship lists for their own accounts. The app is not designed to access private data from other accounts.

## AI-Assisted Summary

AI-powered insights can make a long list easier to understand by summarizing patterns and changes. Treat AI output as a helper, not as a substitute for reviewing the source lists.

## Not Recommended For

- Scraping Instagram profiles
- Accessing private account data
- Replacing official Instagram analytics
- Monitoring people without permission
- Uploading someone else's export without authorization
