# Instagram Following List Viewer

Instagram Detailed List Viewer can be used as an Instagram following list viewer for your own Instagram data export.

## What A Following List Viewer Does

The following list shows accounts your Instagram account follows. In the raw export, this list can be difficult to read or compare. A following list viewer helps you turn that file into a searchable report.

Use the following list viewer to:

- Review accounts you follow
- Search usernames and display names when available
- Compare following against followers
- Find accounts you follow that do not appear in your follower list
- Compare following snapshots over time
- Summarize cleanup opportunities with AI-powered insights

## How To Open Your Following List

1. Request your Instagram data export.
2. Download and extract the archive.
3. Open the export in Instagram Detailed List Viewer.
4. Choose the following list or the relationship summary.
5. Use search and filters to inspect the accounts.

## Following List Cleanup

A following list cleanup is usually safest when you work from your own export and verify each account before taking action. The app can help organize the review, but you decide what to do inside Instagram.

## Follow-Back Comparison

When both follower and following lists are loaded, the app can compare them. This produces two useful views:

- Accounts you follow that do not appear in the follower list
- Followers who do not appear in the following list

These reports depend on the export files you provided. If a file is missing or incomplete, the comparison may be incomplete.
