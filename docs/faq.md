# FAQ

## What Is Instagram Detailed List Viewer?

Instagram Detailed List Viewer is a closed-source app for opening Instagram data export lists and turning followers, following, and account relationship files into readable reports.

## Is This An Instagram Data Export Viewer?

Yes. The app is designed to help you inspect Instagram export files, especially follower and following lists.

## Does It Need My Instagram Password?

No. The app is designed around downloaded export files, not Instagram login credentials.

## Is It Affiliated With Instagram Or Meta?

No. Instagram Detailed List Viewer is independent software and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to Instagram, Meta, Facebook, or Threads.

## Can It Show Who Does Not Follow Me Back?

It can compare your following list against your follower list from the export you provide. Accounts in following but not in followers can be shown as not following back.

## Can It Show Who Unfollowed Me?

A single export usually cannot prove a full historical unfollower timeline. To analyze changes, save exports from different dates and compare the snapshots.

## Can It Analyze Private Accounts?

It analyzes the files you provide from your own export. It does not bypass privacy controls or access private data from other accounts.

## Does It Work With JSON?

JSON is the recommended format when Instagram gives you a choice. See [file formats](file-formats.md).

## Does It Work Offline?

Check the release notes for the specific build. File viewing may be local in many app designs, but AI-powered insight features may require network access if they use an external AI provider.

## Is The App Open Source?

No. The app is closed source. This repository contains public documentation, support information, and release notes.

## Where Can I Download It?

Check [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/singaporeandurian/instagram-detailed-list-viewer/releases) for public builds when they are available.
