## What The App Does

Instagram exports are useful, but they are not very readable. A single account export can include JSON files, lists of accounts, timestamps, profile URLs, and relationship categories spread across folders. Instagram Detailed List Viewer gives those files a clearer interface so you can search, filter, compare, and summarize your own data.

The app is not an Instagram scraper. It does not ask for your Instagram password. It is not official Instagram analytics. It works from the export files you provide.
